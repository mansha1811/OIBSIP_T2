Biography
"Rajesh Khanna, born on December 29, 1942, in Amritsar, Punjab, was an iconic
Indian actor and film producer. He rose to immense fame and popularity in the
1970s, earning the title of "First Superstar" of Bollywood. Rajesh Khanna starred in
numerous successful films, captivating audiences with his charm and romantic
performances. He holds the record for the most consecutive solo hit films in Hindi
cinema, with 15 consecutive solo hit movies between 1969 and 1971. Rajesh
Khanna's charismatic persona, unique style, and memorable dialogues made him an
enduring symbol of Hindi cinema's golden era. He passed away on July 18, 2012,
leaving behind a remarkable legacy."
